# LLVM Qualification Group’s May 2026 Sync-Up Agenda

The next LLVM Qualification Group sync-up will be an open working session. No dedicated topic is planned this month; we will use the time to share a few short updates, follow up on ongoing actions, and discuss any questions or topics raised by participants. Updates include a new membership request received by the group, the new LLVM working groups repository, current documentation/artifact work, and next steps for the group. As usual, everyone interested in the Qualification Group’s work is welcome to join the discussion.
